Our Story

Ghana's Trusted Auto Link
Since 2009

VicDavis Auto Link was founded with a single mission: to give Ghanaians access to premium quality vehicles at fair prices. As official partners of Copart and IAAI — America's largest auto auction platforms — we bring the USA car market directly to your doorstep in Accra.

From our base opposite Dansoman Police Station, we have imported over 500 vehicles, built lasting relationships with thousands of Ghanaian families, and earned our reputation as the most trusted auto import link in the country.
